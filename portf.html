Mobile Signal Jammer

A Mobile Signal Jammer is a device that blocks mobile network signals by transmitting interference at the same frequency as cellular communication. It prevents mobile phones from making or receiving calls, texts, or data connections within a specified range. These jammers are commonly used in restricted areas like examination halls, military zones, and confidential meetings. .
